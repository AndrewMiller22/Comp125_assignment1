About True North Smokehouse

Our Story

True North Smokehouse was inspired by the bold traditions of Texas BBQ and the natural flavors of Canada. Our goal is to bring together slow-smoked meats, maple-infused sauces, and Canadian-inspired dishes in a welcoming, rustic atmosphere.

From brisket and ribs to creative northern specialties, every dish is prepared with care, quality ingredients, and a passion for authentic smokehouse cooking.

Our Vision

We aim to create a place where friends and families can gather to enjoy comforting meals, celebrate Canadian culture, and experience BBQ in a unique and modern way.
